About SkiStats

SkiStats is a platform designed to help skiers explore ski resorts across the U.S. through clean visualizations and sortable data.

Data Sources

The resort data is compiled from public sources including SkiResort.info, resort websites, and various government and tourism databases. We do our best to ensure accuracy, but please verify details directly with the resorts before planning your trip.
